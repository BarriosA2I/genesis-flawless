# Services Catalog — Barrios A2I (Productized Offers)

## 1) Marketing Overlord (Campaign Orchestrator)
**Positioning:** Autonomous campaign management + copy generation across channels.  
**Public price line:** **$199/mo** (the landing page also references a setup fee in the pricing section).  
**Best for:** businesses that need consistent, ongoing marketing output without constant manual effort.

**Typical outputs**
- Campaign plans + channel breakdown (social, email, ads, landing pages)
- Copy variants + hooks + CTAs
- Continuous optimization suggestions based on results

---

## 2) Neural Ad Forge (Video Ad Generation)
**Positioning:** High-conversion video ads generated single-shot from raw text.  
**Public price line:** **$500/VID**  
**Best for:** brands running ads who need speed + volume.

**Typical outputs**
- 1 video ad asset per order (platform-ready MP4)
- Multiple versions possible with additional orders (variants, angles, hooks)

---

## 3) Cinesite Autopilot (Conversion Surface)
**Positioning:** Self-optimizing landing pages that A/B test themselves in real-time.  
**Public price line:** **$1,500**  
**Best for:** businesses that need a high-performing conversion page that improves over time.

**Typical outputs**
- Landing page build + deployment guidance
- Conversion-focused layout + copy + sections
- Optimization plan / experiment roadmap

## 4) Total Command (Custom Systems)

- Support deflection + ticket triage + reporting
- Marketing & sales pipelines
