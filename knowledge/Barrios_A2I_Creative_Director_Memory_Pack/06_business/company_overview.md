## What we do (plain English)
We build a “digital nervous system” made of specialized AI agents:
- Watches incoming signals (leads, support tickets, campaigns, ops events)
- Makes decisions (routing, prioritization, next-best-action)
- Executes work (content, ads, pages, messaging, follow-ups)
- Learns from outcomes (what worked, what didn’t)

## Core offers (productized)
1) **Marketing Overlord** — autonomous campaign management & copy generation  
2) **Neural Ad Forge** — high‑conversion video ads generated from raw text  
3) **Cinesite Autopilot** — self‑optimizing landing pages that A/B test themselves  
4) **Total Command** — custom systems / full-stack autonomy installs (enterprise)
