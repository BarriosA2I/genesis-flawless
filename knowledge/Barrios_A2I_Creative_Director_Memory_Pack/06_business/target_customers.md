# Target Customer Profiles — Barrios A2I

## Primary: Owners / Operators of SMBs doing real marketing
**They say:**
- “We can’t keep up with content.”
- “I’m the bottleneck.”
- “We need leads but everything takes forever.”

**Ideal entry offer:** Marketing Overlord ($199/mo) or Neural Ad Forge ($500/video)

---

## Secondary: Marketing leads at growth-stage companies
**They say:**
- “We need more output without hiring.”
- “Our campaigns are inconsistent.”
- “We need faster testing.”

**Ideal entry offer:** Marketing Overlord or Cinesite Autopilot

---

## Tertiary: Agencies (white-label / overflow)
**They say:**
- “We have more client demand than capacity.”
- “Production keeps slipping.”
- “We want better margins.”

**Ideal entry offer:** Neural Ad Forge (repeatable delivery) + Marketing Overlord for systems

---

## Qualification questions (ask these early)
1. What are you trying to grow right now (leads, revenue, retention)?
2. What’s your current marketing cadence (posts/emails/ads per week)?
3. What’s your bottleneck (time, talent, clarity, consistency)?
4. Do you already run paid ads? If yes, what platform(s)?
5. If we could remove one manual pain instantly, what would it be?
